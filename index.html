What Subtext Does

  • Summarizes articles into concise bullet points
  • Flags emotionally loaded language in context
  • Provides quick links to fact-checks and related coverage
  • Helps you cross-reference sources and dig deeper

How It Works

Subtext is a browser extension that stays idle until you open the side panel on an article page. Click "Analyze" and Subtext sends the article to its analysis service, returning structured results in seconds.

Designed to support careful reading, not replace your judgment.
